Here are some details and events during the life of Iroh

  • The Oldest son of Firelord Azulon, Iroh was once the Crown Prince of The Fire Nation until his younger brother Ozai was chosen.
  • He had a great love for Tea.
  • He was a retired General in the Fire Nation Army.
  • Grand Master in The Order of the White Lotus.
  • He was also known at the Dragon of the West because he was able to breathe fire and the claim that he slayed the last dragons.
  • His only child name Lu Ten was killed in battle.
  • During the first siege of Ba Sing Se of the Hundred Year War He Laid Seige to the Earth Nation capital for 600 days Breaching the outer wall a feat that had never been done before.
  • His only son was killed during the Siege of Ba Sing Se which caused him to lose the will to fight and gave up the enter seige all together.
  • After losing his son Iroh lost all desire for power retiring from the Army and traveling the world.
  • During his Travels around the world Iroh went to the spirit world in search of his son where he learned a greater understanding of life.
  • After His Nephew was Banished by brother the Firelord, He followed him on a quest to find the long lost Avatar to return home.
  • He went against the Fire Nation during the siege of at the Northern Water tribe after the commanding general killed the moon spirit.
  • After his betrayal he was on the run for several months where he lived among the earth kingdom where he became the owner of a tea shop before he was betrayed by his nephew and imprisoned.
  • During his imprisonment in the Fire Nation he escaped during a solar eclipse where FireBenders lost all their bending abilities.
  • After his escape from prison he met with the members of the secret Order of the White Lotus to lay siege to the Ba Sing Se.
  • During the battle of Sozin` Comet he liberated Ba Sing Se from the Fire Nation.
  • After the defeat of Firelord Ozai by the Avatar His Nephew Zuko became the new Firelord he remained in The Earth Kingdom to run his tea shop instead of returning to The Fire Nation.
  • He Eventually returned to the Fire Nation and became interm Firelord as Zuko Set off on a quest to find his long lost Mother.
  • Feeling that he had done all he could in the material world, Iroh chose to ascend to the Spirit World, leaving his mortal body behind and letting his soul make a new home there, where he made friends with many spirits, and also constructed a new tea shop.

"it is usually best to admit mistakes when they occur, and to seek to restore honor." --Iroh.
